    • Pahang

    Pahang is a large state in Peninsular Malaysia. It's known for its mountains, rainforests and beaches, including the white sands and coral reefs of Tioman Island off the east coast. The state capital, Kuantan, is a major seaport. In the west, the gentle hills of the Cameron Highlands include resorts, tea plantations and terraced farms, and the village of Fraser’s Hill resembles an English hamlet. Pahang (Malay pronunciation: [paˈhaŋ]; Jawi:, Pahang Hulu Malay: Paha, Pahang Hilir Malay: Pahaeng, Ulu Tembeling Malay: Pahaq), officially Pahang Darul Makmur with the Arabic honorific Darul Makmur is a sultanate and a federal state of Malaysia. It is the third largest state in Malaysia, largest state in Peninsular Malaysia and ranking on the ninth of popularity in Malaysia. The state borders the Malaysian states of Kelantan and Terengganu to the north, Perak, Selangor and Negeri Sembilan to the west and Johor to the south, with the South China Sea is to the east.

    Mossy Forest Cameron Highlands
    ENCHANTING ECOSYSTEM
    Deemed as the oldest forest in Malaysia (around 200 million years old), the enchanting Mossy Forest spreads around 914 hectares of green sceneries, located near to the summit of Mount Brinchang, Pahang.A swampy ride up with a Land Cruiser will bring you 2,031 meters above sea level; the highest drive up on a mountain in Southeast Asia. Along the 20-minute drive, you will be able to set your eyes on one of the most beautiful tea plantations in South East Asia, the Tea Plantation of Sungai Palas.
    Operating hours: 0800-2130
    Ticket pricing: Adult: RM10 Child: RM5
    Address: Strawberry Park Resort, Brinchang, 39000 Brinchang, Pahang

    Ikan Patin Tempoyak
    If you love getting fresh durian delivered to your doorstep or savouring yummy durian desserts, there's a chance that you'll love ikan patin tempoyak too.
    It is made of out ikan patin, which is a freshwater fish and served with thick tempoyak, which is a fermented durian gravy.
    Location: Gobang Maju Patin Tempoyak
    Operating hour: Open daily; 1030-2230
    Address: 46, Jalan Triang, 28000 Temerloh, Pahang
